DigitalBookNumber (DBN)

DigitalBookNumber (DBN) is an international identification number that digital books can have for free.

How to register my digital book?

To register your digital book, you must go to "Request my DBN", where you must answer the form.

I don't want to wait for the review process, what can I do?

You can click on "Generator", it will open a page that generates a BDBN (Basic DigitalBookNumber) for your digital book.

Is it really free?

Yes, totally.
