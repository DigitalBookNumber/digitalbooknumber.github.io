Introduction

DigitalBookNumber ("we," "our," or "company") is concerned about the privacy of our users and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from DigitalBookNumber users.

Information Collected

DigitalBookNumber collects personal information from users via the registration form, which includes the following information: Name of the book, Number of pages, Authors of the book, Publisher of the book, Publication date of the book, and the IP address of the device used by the user. This information is stored in our database and is used to identify and catalog digital books through a unique identification number.
